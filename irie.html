Hello Awesome people!

More than a year ago, I decided to take my wellness even more seriously than I was earlier in my forties. After losing my mother and brother within two years of each other to the dreadful and destructive lifestyle disease DIABETES, I now treat my overall wellness as a matter of life or death. At this juncture, I must live for them as I feel as though they got snuffed out way earlier than they should. This role of Health Coach has taught me that every moment filled with doubt, anxiety or pressure can be used as a catalyst for a fresh start. You are the only one responsible for your health and wellness. I engage my clients though TRANSFORMATION - A MIND AND BODY APPROACH TO WELLNESS. Here is where I come in; I listen, support, plan and assist you in executing your goals in order to achieve balance; optimal health and wellness.
